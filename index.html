About Penina:
Penina is a specialized layout and text-generation tool designed to create highly accessible, vowelized Hebrew reading materials. It offers granular control over typography, spacing, and visual cues to support diverse reading needs.

Credits:
• Developed by David Israel
• Vowelization powered by the Dicta API
• Dyslexia-friendly typography via Dyslexic-Kriah
